About The Chamber

About The Chamber

History of the Ethiopian Chamber of Commerce The first chamber of commerce in the history of Ethiopia was established pursuant to General Notice No. 90/1947 which clearly defined the functions and duties of chamber and membership was voluntary. In 1971 the Ethiopian Chamber of Commerce (ECC) has been reorganized with branches in Asmara, Dire Dawa, Jimma, Gonder & Nazareth. Following the change of government in 1974, Proclamation No. 148/1974, which was basically a public law, was promulgated and the Ethiopian chamber of commerce was restructured in line with centrally planned economic policy of the then government. Membership had become mandatory. In 2003, Proclamation No. 341/2003 the re-constitute of chambers of commerce was based on Developmental Democratic System. Accordingly, in line with this the Ethiopian Chamber of Commerce has been restructured and renamed as the Ethiopian Chamber of Commerce & Sectoral Associations (ECCSA).

Establishment of ECCSA

ECCSA is an Autonomous, Non-for-Profit, Non-Partisan, Private Sector institution and membership based organization. ECCSA was established with the aim of projecting trade and investment, inter alia, in a bid to creating a vibrant private sector and business friendly environment, working in cooperation with the government, business community, development partners and stakeholders. ECCSA has eighteen members including nine Regional Chambers of Commerce and Sectoral Associations, two City Chambers of Commerce and Sectoral Associations, one National Chamber of Sectoral Association and six Sectoral Associations organized at national level. The higher decision making body at the Chamber system is the General Assembly (GA) which is composed of the representatives of member chambers of commerce and sectoral associations of ECCSA. The GA elects the president, the vice president and nine Board of Directors every two years. A management team led by the secretariat manages the day to day operation of the chamber. ECCSA Objectives

ECCSA Objectives

According to the proclamation 341/2003 article 3, Ethiopian Chamber of Commerce and Sectoral Associations have the following objectives:-

  • - To provide different services to the business community;
  • - To safeguard the overall rights and benefits of its members;
  • - To promote and publicize products and services of the country;
  • - To enhance trade and investment of the country;
  • - To serve as a bridge between the business community and the government.

Mission, Vision, Core Values

Mission: To head the creation of enabling business environment and capacity in which the business community plays a leading role in the economy through evidence based dialogue and partnership.

Vision: To see a dynamic Chamber system proactively responsive towards sustainable private sector growth

Strategic Priority Areas

A stronger and influential ECCSA; Be a positive influence on the creation of business friendly environment; The leading voice of the business community in the country; Create a stronger and influential Chambers/Associations; Raise the private sector profile. Our Services

The Legal & Arbitration Service is a department tasked with, as the name itself indicates, affairs relating legal and arbitration. Major services offered (functions) by the Department/Division are:-

Legal Service: Advising the Board of Directors, the Secretariat, departments and divisions; Representing ECCSA at courts of different levels; drafting legal documents; extending advice to members of the business community and give the required support to, specially, those embarking on import/export activities; are chief to mention.

Arbitration Service: Facilitating forum for arbitration, conducting annual workshop on Alternative Dispute Resolution, Renting out Arbitration room for ad hoc arbitration; are some of the services well undertaken by the department.

• Major Clients The major stakeholders (clients) are members of the business community, the government and ECCSA’s staff and members.

• Achievements ECCSA has established an Arbitration center with a designated room with all facilities to carry out the service.

• Partnership The legal and arbitration center works in collaboration with different stakeholders (Government, Lawyers and development partners)

Ethiopian Chamber Academy (ECA) emerged as a national training provider in March 2014. It is destined to serve chamber and association leaders, managers, and professionals to further their professional development, and build their capacity to lead and operate within their organizations. The most notable elements that have contributed to the formation of the ECA include:

• The development of 17 courses and their translation into local vernacular;

• The training of 29 Ethiopian trainers on 13 different topics through two training programs.

• The training received through the International Organization for Management (IOM) training program sponsored by the U.S. Chamber Foundation and a study tour organized in the United States.

In every part of the world building reliable and credible business networks is not an easy task. This is why the Chamber steps in to fill such gaps for the business communities so as to help them grow by creating business links with potential partners to bolster mutual benefits. Business communities can testify to a variety of opportunities that have come their way through international trade fairs organized by the chamber while networking at such events. Interaction and exchange of ideas is an essential ingredient to keeping up with the dynamic and ever-changing face of the business scene and if one is to keep on track the most appropriate mode would be to take advantage of the networking platforms that the chamber offers to its members. The network is being created by organizing and participating in trade fairs, exchange of delegation, B2B, among others.

    Date: October 14, 2023

    Arbaminch, Ethiopia

    በአርባምንጭ ከተማ የንግድና የዘርፍ ማህበራት ምክር ቤት አዘጋጅነት "አድቫንስድ ዲጂታል ማርኬትንግ" ሥልጠና ለከተማው የንግድ ማህበረሰብና ባለ ድርሻ አካላት ተሰጠ። አርባምንጭ፡ሰኔ 22/2015 ዓ/ም፡ አርባምንጭ ከተማ መ/ኮሚዩኒኬሽን የአርባምንጭ ከተማ ምክትል ከንቲባና የንግድና ገበያ ልማት ጽ/ቤት ኃላፊ አቶ ምህረቱ ተሰማ በመድረኩ ተገኝተው  የሚሰጠው ሥልጠና ሰው አንድ ሀገር ላይ  የሚሰጠውን የንግድ አገልግሎት ዘርፍ እንዴት በዲጂታል መንገድ  መስራት ይቻላል  የሚለውን የሚወስን ነው ሲሉ ገልጸዋል። ከተማዋ እጅግ ፈጣን በሆነ ዕድገት ላይ ያለች ከተማ በመሆኗ በንግዱ ዘርፍ የተሰማሩ የንግዱ ማህበረሰብ አካላት አገልግሎት አሰጣጣቸው ቀልጣፋና ዘመናዊ መሆን አለበት ብለዋል። የንግዱ ማህበረሰብ አገልግሎታቸውን በዲጂታል መንገድ ማስደገፍ ሲችሉ ለሚሰሩት አገልግሎት ተደራሽነትን የሚያፋጥን ፥አገልግሎታቸው ቀልጣፋ እንዲሆን የሚያስችል እና የሚሰሩት ሥራ ከማንኛውም አሉታዊ ተጽእኖ ነጻ እንዲሆን የሚያደርግ ነው ሲሉም ሀላፊው ተናግረዋል። አቶ ቦጋለ ቢታኔ የአርባምንጭ ከተማ የንግድና የዘርፍ ማህበራት ምክርቤት ፕሬዝዳንት በአሁኑ ሰዓት ዓለም ከዘልማዳዊ ከሆነው  የግብይት ሥርዓት ውጪ እየሆነ እንዳለና የአርባምንጭ ከተማ ደግሞ  የአለም ማህበረሰብ ትኩረት እየሳበች የምትገኝ ከተማ በመሆኗ በርካታ የሀገር ውስጥና የውጪ ቱርስቶች ወደ ከተማዋ ስለሚመጡ የሚፈልጉትን አገልግሎት በቀላሉ ማግኘት እንዲችሉ የንግድ ዘርፍ ላይ የተሰማሩ  በኦንላይን ማርኬትንግ አገልግሎታቸውን በማስተዋወቅ ቱሪስቶች የሰርቪሱ ተጠቃሚ እንዲሆኑ የማድረግ ሥራ መሥራት አለባቸው ብለዋል። የከተማው የንግድና የዘርፍ ማህበራት ም/ቤቱ ከያዛቸው ዓላማዎች ዋነኛው የዘመነ፣ የሰለጠነና የነቃ የንግድ ማህበረሰብ መፍጠርና በዘመናዊ ሚዲያ የዲጂታል ፕላት ፎርም እንዲጠቀሙ ማድረግ ነው ብለዋል። ከዚህ ሥልጠና በኋላ ሰልጣኞች የዲጂታል ፕላትፎርሙን በመቀላቀል የፌስቡክ ቢዝነስ አካውንት፣ ኢንስታግራም ቢዝነስ አካውንት፣ ዋትዛፕ ቢዝነስ አካውንት እና ሌሎችን የቢዝነስ አካውንቶች በመፍጠር በተለየ ደረጃ ምርትንና አገልግሎትን ከማስተዋወቅ ባለፈ ከተማዋንና የተለያዩ የአገልግሎት ሰጪ ተቋማት በከተማዋ እንዳሉ የማስተዋወቅ ሥራ መሥራት ይጠበቅባቸዋል ሲሉም አሳስበዋል። ሥልጠናው በተለያዩ ዙሮች ቀጣይነት እንደሚኖረውም ተገልጿል።
